FAQ

  1. My song was uploaded without my permission! Can I have it removed?


    Certainly. Please flag it and our moderators will take care of it.

  2. I have a large collection (like, gigabytes) of songs. Can I upload it here?


    Please do not upload your large collection of songs.

  3. I don't think _____________ song belongs on this website. What should I do?


    Please flag it and let our moderators take care of the rest.

  4. How do I become a moderator?


    Our moderators are there to enforce the uploading agreements. Flagging songs according to the uploading rules is a good first step to contribute to the website. If we need moderators, we will look for these people first.

  5. Can I redistribute the songs on this website?


    All songs are uploaded under the Creative Commons NonCommercial License. This means you are free to share and adapt the material as you would like as long as you do not use it for any commercial purposes.

  6. Can I remove the download button from my songs?


    No. Please only upload the songs that you want allow others to enjoy through any medium.
